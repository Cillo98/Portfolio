Background

Middle school

He started off in this desolate middle school surrounded by trees, little houses and monkeys. Alright, maybe monkeys is too far. Anyway, it was a pretty nice school with lots of things to do! There was maths, literature, maths, literature, maths... And yes, stop wondering, the dude we're talking about is that weirdo with long hair and a purple T-shirt!!

High school

Finally, he left for high school. Yes, he had a good time there, made friends, coded stuff, played with barbies and all those things everyone does at that age, yes. However, most of his time was spent on a very, very specific task... Developing a Space Invaders using Pascal! He also learned some Chinese at that time, but that's a story for another time...

Chinese High School

Yes, he went through it too. Hard to believe, but true story! Of course, if we do not consider that it was all in Chinese!

University

And here is him now, at last in University (yes, somehow he made it!). These days he's having a lot of fun playing around with his co...des, trying to carefully change CSS lines of a website without the whole thing exploding (and miserably failing), trying even harder to make his Java arrays work without throwing random IndexOutOfBounds exceptions and, mostly, trying to figure out what that unholy graph represents.
